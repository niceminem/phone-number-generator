About UK Phone Numbers

UK phone numbers follow specific formatting rules with different prefixes for landlines, mobiles, and special services. The UK uses a variable-length numbering system.

Key features of UK phone numbers:

  • Landline Numbers: Begin with 01 or 02 (geographic numbers)
  • Mobile Numbers: Begin with 07 followed by 9 digits
  • Freephone Numbers: Begin with 0800 or 0808
  • Premium Rate: Begin with 09 (premium rate services)
  • Business Rate: Begin with 0845 or 0870
